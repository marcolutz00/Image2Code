THURSDAY, AUGUST 09, 2012
MTB organization ADSVMQ formally hands over trail development duties to Vélo Québec

Breaking news: The Québec mountain biking organization, ADSVMQ, has officially passed its trail development responsibilities to Vélo Québec.

Québec holds tremendous potential for mountain bike tourism, pending trail construction. The efforts of ADSVMQ greatly improved and modernized Québec trails. The anticipation is that Vélo Québec will promote bike tourism and inspire regions to improve their networks. Given Québec's size, many areas hold great trail development potential.

Québec could be a prime mountain biking destination. We understand that the parks are starting to consider mountain bikers as valid park users. National parks, please build dedicated mountain bike trails.
