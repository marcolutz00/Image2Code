RENEW A DOMAIN SERVICES

How to extend the registration of a domain name

  1. To renew your domain name, log in to your web hosting Control Panel using the login button provided below.
  2. After logging in, navigate to the Registered Domains section under the My Domains menu. There, you'll find a list of all domains registered or transferred to your account. Locate the domain you want to renew, check the box next to it, and click the Renew button in the actions toolbar at the top.
  3. This will initiate the domain renewal process just follow the on-screen steps to complete the transaction. In most cases, domain renewals are processed within an hour.
