Export news from Google Sites

It is possible to export news from Google Sites. Here is a step-by-step guide on how you can do this:

1. Log into your Google account.

2. Go to the site where you want to export the news articles.

3. Click on the three dots in the top right corner of the page.

4. Select "Export" from the dropdown menu.

5. The exported files will be downloaded to your computer as a ZIP file.

Please note that not all sites are available for export.
