Welcome

Dr. Emma Ostrovskaya is a qualified and experienced counsellor with a passion for helping individuals and families navigate life's challenges. With a background in both counselling psychology and existential therapy, she offers a unique approach to therapy that is tailored to each individual's unique needs.

Therapy

Dr. Emma Ostrovskaya offers a range of therapy options, including individual therapy, couples therapy, and family therapy. She works with a variety of issues, including anxiety, depression, relationship difficulties, and trauma. Dr. Emma is committed to providing a safe and supportive environment for her clients to explore their thoughts and feelings, and to work towards their goals.
