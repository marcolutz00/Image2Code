May 2011 Hero – U.S. Marine LCpl Alejandro Macias

Home News Programs About Us

by John | May 1, 2011 | Club News

The Round Lake Area Exchange Club Hometown Hero Committee is honored to announce U.S. Marine Lance Corporal Alejandro Macias as the May hero. This month’s sponsor is Collision Solution located in Hainesville.

Macias was registered at the Clubs site, www.rlaexchange.org/hero, by his very proud mother, Victoria L. Macias. The May hero, a Round Lake Park resident, graduated in 2010 from Round Lake Senior High School.
