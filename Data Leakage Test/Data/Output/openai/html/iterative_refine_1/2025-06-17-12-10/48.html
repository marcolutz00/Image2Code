Oranges now costing $20 in yr 2022 → $19.66 in yr 2023

Orange Prices, 2022-2023 ($20)

Per the U.S. Bureau of Labor Statistics, costs of oranges are 1.69% lower in the yr 2023 versus 2022 (a $0.34 change in USD value).

Between 2022 & 2023: Oranges had an avg. increase rate of -1.69% each year. To explain, oranges at $20 in the yr 2022 would now be $19.66 in yr 2023 to buy same amount. Compared to the total increase rate of 3.97% during this same time, the change for oranges was much more decreased.

During the yr 2022: Costs changed by 11.58%, that is much more than the typical change each year for oranges during the 2022-2023 time frame. Compared to all item increase in the yr 2022 (8%), the change for oranges was greatly higher.
