Production Function

The production function is a mathematical representation of the relationship between inputs and outputs.

Capital

Capital refers to any resources or assets that are used in the process of production. This includes physical items such as buildings, machinery, and equipment.

Labor

Labor refers to the human effort that is involved in the process of production. This includes skilled workers and unskilled laborers.

Entrepreneurship

Entrepreneurship refers to the ability to identify and take advantage of new opportunities. This includes innovators, organizers, and risk takers.
