Prices for Citrus Fruits, 2022-2023 ($20)

According to the U.S. Bureau of Labor Statistics, prices for citrus fruits are 1.69% lower in 2023 versus 2022 (a $0.34 difference in value).

Between 2022 and 2023: Citrus fruits experienced an average inflation rate of -1.69% per year. In other words, citrus fruits costing $20 in the year 2022 would cost $19.66 in 2023 for an equivalent purchase.

In the year 2022: Pricing changed by 11.58%, which is significantly above the average yearly change for citrus fruits during the 2022-2023 time period. Compared to inflation for all items in 2022 (8%), inflation for citrus fruits was much higher.

Adjust citrus fruits prices for inflation

Start with the inflation rate formula:

CPI in 2023 / CPI in 2022 * 2022 USD value = 2023 USD value

Then plug in historical CPI values from above. The CPI for Citrus fruits was 299.766 in the year 2022 and 294.714 in 2023:

294.714 / 299.766 * $20 = $19.66

Therefore, according to U.S. Bureau of Labor Statistics, $20 in 2022 has the same "purchasing power" as $19.66 in 2023 (in the CPI category of Citrus fruits).
