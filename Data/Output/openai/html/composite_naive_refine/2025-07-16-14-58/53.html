The Indian Oil Corporation was set up in which year?

A - 1964 B - 1965 C - 1966 D - 1970
Answer : A
Explanation
Indian Oil Corporation is an Indian state owned oil and gas company primarily headquartered in New Delhi. It is the largest commercial oil company in the country.
