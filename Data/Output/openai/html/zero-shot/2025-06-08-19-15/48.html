Check oranges values for increasing

Begin the increased cost form:

CPI for yr 2023 / CPI for yr 2022 * 2022 USD worth = 2023 USD worth

Then set the old CPI numbers from above. The CPI for Oranges was 299.766 in the yr 2022 and also 294.714 in yr 2023:

294.714 / 299.766 * $20 = $19.66
