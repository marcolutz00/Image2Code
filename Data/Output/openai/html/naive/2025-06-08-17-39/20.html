Third Annual MIIA Clay Target Competition

The ATA Comp Fund proudly supported and participated in the annual clay shoot held by Montgomery Independent Insurance Agents at Lower Wetumpka Shotgun Sports. In its third year, the MIIA was able to raise proceeds for the Ministries of Hope. Always a blast, MIIA!
