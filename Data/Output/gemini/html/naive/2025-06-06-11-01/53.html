In which year was the Indian Oil Corporation established?
A - 1964
B - 1965
C - 1966
D - 1970
Answer : A
Explanation

Indian Oil Corporation is a state-owned Indian oil and gas enterprise, with its primary headquarters located in New Delhi. It stands as the largest commercial oil company in the nation.
