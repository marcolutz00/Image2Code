Every person should possess the right to not just accept the idea of hell for the wicked and heaven for the chosen. Every person will get the right to pick hell instead of heaven. That creates separation from the Holy See, simply because democratic thinking shall never dominate holy writing. Still, it is true for John Paul no individual holds the right-democratic and otherwise-to a moral transgression;....-p. 287.

Stated differently, Martin claims that, similar to the former popes, right now the papacy does not consider freedom as a choice, and might not grant that as a role in the future world structure they seek.
