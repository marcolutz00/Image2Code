Prices for Citrus Fruits, 2022-2023 ($20)

According to the U.S. Bureau of Labor Statistics, prices for citrus fruits are 1.69% lower in 2023 versus 2022 (a $0.34 difference in value).

Between 2022 and 2023: Citrus fruits experienced an average inflation rate of -1.69% per year. In other words, citrus fruits costing $20 in the year 2022 would cost $19.66 in 2023 for an equivalent purchase. Compared to the overall inflation rate of 3.97% during this same period, inflation for citrus fruits was significantly lower.

In the year 2022: Pricing changed by 11.58%, which is significantly above the average yearly change for citrus fruits during the 2022-2023 time period. Compared to inflation for all items in 2022 (8%), inflation for citrus fruits was much higher.
