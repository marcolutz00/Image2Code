Local MTB group hands off trail development to Cycling Quebec

The word is that the regional mountain bike trail organization has officially transferred their trail building duties to Cycling Quebec today.

Québec is perfect for mountain bike tourism if trails are constructed. The recent projects moved mountains and modernized trails. The expectation is that Cycling Quebec will use this tourism angle and encourage regions to improve their networks. Given Québec's size, many regions are suitable for trail expansion.

Québec could become a great destination for mountain biking. We understand that the parks are starting to consider mountain bikers as valid park users. Let's hope the parks start building real trails.
